e-Entity

E-entity is based on entity recognition software and existing linked entity datasets from the Linked Data and Open Data. Its purpose is to locate and classify items in text into pre-defined categories such as the names of persons, organizations, locations etc.

e-Translation

E-Translation is based on cloud machine translation services for building custom machine translation systems. Its purpose is to automatically translate content from a source language to a target language.

e-Link

COMING SOON. E-link is based on the technology Natural Language Processing Interchange Format (NIF) and Linked Open Vocabularies. Its purpose is to enrich content with additional information.

e-Terminology

E-terminology is based on cloud terminology services for terminology management and terminology annotation web service to annotate terminology in ITS 2.0 enriched content. Its purpose is to identify terms automatically and provide domain information + translation suggestion.
